What is the concept behind the sports meme coin marketplace?

The sports meme coin marketplace is designed to release new sports-related meme coins on a weekly basis. Each coin represents a different sports player and has a uniform total supply of 1 billion (1B) coins. The marketplace aims to build a fun ecosystem where users can trade and engage with meme coins based on their favorite sports players. All coins will have 100% circulating supply upon release, ensuring transparency and a true reflection of market demand.

How does the initial coin offering work?

Each sports meme coin will be launched on the pump.fun platform. At launch, the team will release the coin with 1.5 SOL (Solana), which constitutes around 5% of the total supply. This initial allocation is designed to kickstart the coin's circulation and provide liquidity for trading. The remaining 95% of the supply will be made available to the community immediately, ensuring fair and equitable access to all participants.

What measures are in place to ensure transparency and fairness?

To ensure transparency and fairness, every sports meme coin will be released with 100% of its supply in circulation from the start. This means there are no pre-mined or reserved coins held back by the team or insiders. By adopting a uniform total supply of 1 billion coins for each player, the marketplace fosters equal opportunity for all users and avoids any potential manipulation of coin availability or value.

How is the value of each sports meme coin determined?

The value of each sports meme coin is determined by its market cap, which is calculated based on the total supply of 1 billion coins and the current market price per coin. Since all coins have the same total supply and are fully circulating upon release, their price dynamics will accurately reflect their market capitalization. This system provides a straightforward and transparent method for evaluating the relative worth of each meme coin in the marketplace.
